About Mohbad

Aloba Promise Ilerioluwa Oladimeji, popularly known as Mohbad (Imole), is a songwriter, performer, rapper and singer. Rising to fame in 2019, following series of freestyle and later Hit songs like KPK, Feel Good, Back Side, Ronaldo, and lots more. Mohbad is in the business of serving great music and is actively on a roll. Mohbad is well placed, not only within the street pop niche, but as a contributor to the growth of Afrobeats...
